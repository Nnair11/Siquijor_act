Siquijor

Is a tiny, mystical island province in the Central Visayas, famously known as the Philippines' "Island of Fire" due to its folklore, fireflies, and traditional healers (mananambal). A popular eco-tourism destination offering white sand beaches, coral-rich diving, waterfalls, and a relaxed, affordable travel experience.

Things to Do

Unforgettable Island Experiences

Snorkeling & Diving

Siquijor's coral-rich waters host sea turtles, vibrant reef fish, and stunning coral gardens. Dive sites like Tubod Marine Sanctuary are among the Philippines' finest.

Island Circumnavigation

Ride the scenic 76 km coastal road by motorbike or tricycle. Dramatic sea views, mountain passes, colorful villages, and hidden coves all in one epic day trip.

Folk Healing Culture

Visit local mananambal (folk healers) during Holy Week's Healing Festival. Traditional remedies, potions, and rituals have been practiced here for centuries.
